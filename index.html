Our Tea Heritage

Since 1962, we have been dedicated to bringing the finest teas from the lush gardens of Assam and Darjeeling to tea lovers worldwide. Our journey began with a simple passion for quality tea and has evolved into a commitment to excellence in every cup.

We work directly with tea estates to ensure ethical sourcing and sustainable farming practices, preserving both the environment and the rich tradition of Indian tea cultivation. Every leaf is handpicked at its peak and processed with care to maintain its authentic flavor and aroma.
